Our Mission

At RetailerBoost, we believe that every retailer deserves access to world-class Google Ads expertise without the risk of upfront costs. Our mission is to democratize digital advertising by making performance-based campaigns accessible to merchants of all sizes.

We handle everything - from campaign setup and funding to optimization and reporting - so you can focus on what you do best: running your business.

Why We're Different from Traditional Agencies

Traditional PPC agencies charge monthly retainers and require you to fund your own advertising budget. If campaigns underperform, you still pay the agency fee and lose your ad spend. The financial risk sits entirely with you.

RetailerBoost flips this model. We are ad spend investors, not consultants. We take on the financial risk, fund the campaigns, and only charge commission on actual sales. This fundamentally changes the relationship - we win when you win, and we lose when campaigns don't perform.

Many retailers use both approaches in parallel. Agencies are excellent for strategic product launches, seasonal pushes, and brand campaigns where you want direct control. RetailerBoost provides incremental, risk-free sales on top of whatever else you're already doing. Both models are valid and complementary.
